--- layout: default title: Sydney Williams ---

I was born and raised in the small town of Morro Bay (pictured below) on the Central Coast of California. After completing high school, I attended the Illinois Institute of Technology for my undergraduate education in biomedical engineering. During that time, I spent 6 months studying abroad at the School of Telecommunications Engineering (ETSIT) at the Polytechnic University of Madrid (UPM) in Spain. After obtaining my bachelor's degree, I entered the biomedical engineering Ph.D. program at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor.

Outside of research, I enjoy playing and listening to music, cooking, practicing hot yoga, and dancing. For my first two years of grad school, I was a member of the UM All-Female Bollywood Fusion team, Michigan TAAL, and now I practice ballet dance. I also served as Co-President of the Biomedical Engineering Graduate Student Council which plans academic/professional and social events for the graduate student of the department. I have taken up running for the past five years, and completed the Chicago Marathon in October 2014 and the New York City Marathon in November 2016.
